Feijoada

Description

Feijoada is a traditional Brazilian dish that is made with black beans and pork. It is a hearty and flavorful meal that is perfect for a cold day. Feijoada is typically served with rice, collard greens, and orange slices. It is a popular dish in Brazil and is often enjoyed at family gatherings and celebrations.

  • Preparation time: 30 minutes
  • Cooking time: 2 hours
  • Servings: 6

Ingredients

  • 1 lb of black beans
  • 1 lb of pork shoulder
  • 1 lb of smoked sausage
  • 1 lb of bacon
  • 1 onion, chopped
  • 4 cloves of garlic, minced
  • 2 bay leaves
  • 1 orange, sliced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Directions

  1. Soak the black beans in water overnight.
  2. Drain the beans and place them in a large pot with enough water to cover them.
  3. Add the pork shoulder, smoked sausage, bacon, onion, garlic, and bay leaves to the pot.
  4. Bring the mixture to a boil, then reduce the heat and simmer for 2 hours, or until the beans are tender.
  5. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  6. Remove the bay leaves and serve the feijoada with rice, collard greens, and orange slices.
  7. Enjoy!
