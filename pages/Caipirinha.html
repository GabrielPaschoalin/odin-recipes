Caipirinha

Description

Caipirinha is a traditional Brazilian cocktail made with cachaça, sugar, and lime. It is a refreshing and tangy drink that is perfect for a hot day. Caipirinha is a popular drink in Brazil and is often enjoyed at parties and celebrations.

  • Preparation time: 5 minutes
  • Servings: 1

Ingredients

  • 2 oz of cachaça
  • 1 lime, cut into wedges
  • 2 teaspoons of sugar
  • Ice

Directions

  1. Muddle the lime wedges and sugar in a glass.
  2. Add the cachaça and stir well.
  3. Fill the glass with ice.
  4. Enjoy!
