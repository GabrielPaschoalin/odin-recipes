Brigadeiro

Description

Brigadeiro is a great Brazilian dessert similar to bonbons that's extremely scrumptious and delicious. This sweet is loved here in Brazil. Try it! You can also make it in the microwave; just remember to stir every minute.

  • Preparation time: 15 minutes
  • Cooking time: 10 minutes
  • Servings: 20

Ingredients

  • 1 can of sweetened condensed milk
  • 1 tablespoon of butter
  • 4 tablespoons of chocolate powder
  • Chocolate sprinkles

Directions

  1. Put the condensed milk, butter, and chocolate powder in a pan.
  2. Cook over medium heat, stirring constantly.
  3. When the mixture thickens and starts to peel off the bottom of the pan, turn off the heat.
  4. Let it cool.
  5. Grease your hands with butter and shape the balls.
  6. Pass the balls in the chocolate sprinkles.
  7. Place the balls in small paper cups.
  8. Enjoy!
